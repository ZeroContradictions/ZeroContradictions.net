About the Author, Zero Contradictions

Hello, and welcome to my blog. My screen name is Zero Contradictions. Some info about me:

  • I’ve lived my whole life in the United States, and I am in my mid 20s. My native language is Western American English, but I can also use Spanish and Esperanto at a B1-B2 fluency level.
  • My ancestry is half European (mostly German), and half Japanese and Cantonese. I look more European than Asian, and unlike most hapas, I have blue eyes since both of my parents are also hapas who each carried a copy of the genes needed for blue eyes from their European ancestors.
  • Three of my great grandfathers were immigrants who migrated to North America to start their own businesses. My Japanese great grandfather migrated to Vancouver to start a fishing company and a construction company, my German great grandfather (direct paternal line) started a lumber mill in Arkansas and a farm in North Dakota, and my Cantonese great grandfather started a Cantonese restaurant in New York City. And then my fourth great grandfather was already born in the United States and was a bus driver and WWI soldier. As far as I know, all of my Asian ancestors had arranged marriages, and my Japanese ancestors were rich landlords. My Japanese great grandmother was a Buddhist from Kyoto and a college professor, which was especially rare for a woman during the early 1900s.
  • I am on the Autism, ADHD, and OCD spectrums, which I inherited mainly from my maternal grandmother, who had Scottish, German, Swiss German, and Swiss French ancestry. My ADHD-C was out of control until I first got medicated, and my social anxiety when I was younger was so bad that I never had an interactive conversation with anyone outside my intermediate family until I was 14 years old. My older brother has severe autism, and this was one of the clues to how I figured out that I’m neurodivergent. While neurodivergence can be very troubling for some people, I view each of my conditions as being a double-edged sword rather than complete disadvantages.
  • I started taking my first college courses when I was 16 years old, I finished my General Studies AA when I was 18 years old, and I finished a Mathematics BS and a Linguistics BA when I was 22 years old. I almost finished a CS minor too, except for the last course.
  • I started seriously thinking about philosophy when I was 19 years old after a devastating breakup had put me into an existential crisis by making me depressed and unsure of how to move on with my life.
  • The main reason why I created this blog is so that I can have a place to put my thoughts out there, and can find like-minded people to talk to. I launched this blog in November 2022, and it is meant to display and explain my own philosophy and all my original philosophical content. My philosophy has many similarities to Blithering Genius’s philosophy, hence why there are many links to his blog and Youtube channel on this website, but my content often focuses on different topics than his stuff.
